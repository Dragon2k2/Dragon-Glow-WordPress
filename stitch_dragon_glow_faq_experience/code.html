The Concierge

Guidance tailored to the discerning individual. Discover insights into our quiet luxury philosophy, bespoke formulations, and the ethereal radiance of Dragon Glow.

How is the 'Dragon Glow' distinct from typical highlighters?

expand_more
Our formulation shuns chunky glitter and synthetic mica. Instead, we utilize an proprietary blend of micro-crushed ethereal minerals suspended in a translucent botanical serum. This creates a refractive, "glass skin" effect that appears to emanate from within, capturing the essence of quiet luxury rather than superficial shine.

Are the botanical ingredients ethically sourced?

expand_more
Absolutely. We partner exclusively with sustainable micro-farms dedicated to regenerative agriculture. Every botanical active is traceable, ensuring zero exploitation and minimal ecological footprint while maximizing efficacy.

Can the serums be layered with retinol?

expand_more
Our collection is designed for gentleness and barrier repair. While safe to use alongside retinol, we recommend applying our restorative serums on alternating nights or in the morning to prevent overwhelming the skin's delicate ecosystem.

What is the shelf life of the bespoke formulations?

expand_more
Given our commitment to eliminating synthetic preservatives, our formulations possess an optimal freshness window of 6 months once opened. We encapsulate our products in UV-protective, dark glass vessels to preserve their vitality.
